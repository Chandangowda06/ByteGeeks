Welcome to our project!

We are excited to put a strong emphasis on the customer assistant aspect of our project in the Cognizant Hackathon 2023. Our aim is to create a virtual assistant that will act as a reliable and knowledgeable guide for customers throughout their shopping journey

Problem Statement

In today's society, women often experience discomfort, anxiety, and a lack of empowerment when navigating public spaces, including shopping malls. It is crucial to create an inclusive and supportive environment that ensures women feel safe, confident, and valued during their shopping experiences

Background information

In the retail industry, catering to the unique needs and emotions of women is of utmost importance. Women constitute a significant customer segment, and providing personalized, empathetic, and empowering experiences for them is not only a moral imperative but also a strategic business move. By understanding and addressing the specific pain points and desires of women, retailers can establish a competitive advantage and build long-lasting relationships.
